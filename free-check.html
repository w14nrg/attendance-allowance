Free Eligibility Check

Answer the questions to see if you may qualify

1. Are you 66 or older? (State Pension age)

2. Do you have any illness, disability or health condition?

3. Do you need help or supervision with washing or bathing?

4. Do you need help or supervision with getting dressed or undressed?

5. Do you need help or supervision with using the toilet?

6. Do you need help or supervision with eating or drinking?

7. Do you need someone to keep an eye on you during the day for your safety?

8. Have these needs lasted (or are expected to last) at least 6 months?

9. Do you live alone or with someone who cannot help with your care needs?

10. Do you have difficulty getting in/out of bed or a chair?

11. Do you need help communicating your needs?

12. Do you need help managing medication or treatments?

13. Do you have difficulty managing money or bills?

14. Do you need help using the telephone or online services?

15. Is your weekly income below £201 (single) or £307 (couple)?

16. Do you have savings or capital over £16,000?

17. Do you have severe difficulty walking or moving around?

18. Do you use any aids or appliances for mobility?

19. Do you live alone or receive no regular help from family/friends with care needs?

20. Any other relevant health or care needs? (optional)
