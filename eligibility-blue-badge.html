What is a Blue Badge?

The Blue Badge scheme provides parking concessions for people with severe mobility problems or certain health conditions. It allows parking closer to destinations in designated spaces.

  • Automatic eligibility: Receiving certain benefits like higher rate mobility DLA, 8+ points PIP moving around, registered blind, War Pensioners' Mobility Supplement, etc.
  • Subject to assessment: Substantial difficulty walking (pain, breathlessness, speed, distance), severe arm disability, child under 3 with medical needs, dangerous behaviour, non-visible conditions causing distress.
  • Who can apply: Drivers or passengers, adults or children, UK residents.
  • Cost: Up to £10 (varies by council), valid 3 years.
  • How it works: Your local council assesses the application – we handle the full process, evidence gathering, and appeals if needed.

Success-based fee from £99 only if approved.
