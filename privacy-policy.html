Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Claim For Me ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website (www.claimfor.me.uk) and our services assisting people over State Pension age with claims for Attendance Allowance, Pension Credit, and Blue Badge applications on a success-based (no-win-no-fee) basis.

We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you submit our "Request a Call Back" form or contact us:

  • Contact details: Full name, postcode, full address (optional), phone number, email address (optional).
  • Sensitive personal data (special category data): Answers to eligibility questions about your age, residency, long-term health conditions/disabilities, personal care needs, and mobility difficulties.

We do not collect payment details as there are no upfront fees.

We may also automatically collect limited technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type) via server logs for security and site improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Assess your potential eligibility for Attendance Allowance, Pension Credit, and/or Blue Badge.
  • Contact you by phone or email to discuss your claim and provide assistance.
  • Prepare and submit benefit applications on your behalf (with your explicit consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for marketing without separate consent, and we never sell your data.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contact details: Legitimate interests (to provide the requested callback and claims assistance service).
  • Sensitive health data: Explicit consent (provided by voluntarily completing the eligibility questions and submitting the form).

You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Where necessary to process your benefit claim (e.g., with the Department for Work and Pensions or local authorities, only with your explicit instruction).
  • With trusted service providers (e.g., email delivery services) bound by strict confidentiality.
  • If required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in the UK/EU. We use appropriate technical and organisational measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) to protect against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Unsuccessful enquiries: Deleted within 6 months.
  • Active/ongoing claims: Retained for the duration of the claim plus 7 years (for legal/tax purposes).
  • You can request deletion at any time (subject to legal obligations).

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)

Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our site uses only essential cookies (if any) and minimal third-party resources (e.g., fonts, icons). No analytics or advertising cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via the website.

10. Contact Us

For privacy queries: Email claimsformeuk@gmail.com or call 020 7122 8324.
